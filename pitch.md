# Improve.ai Pitch

## Problem
People struggle to improve movement quality from video-only instruction because they do not get timely, specific, and measurable feedback.

## Product
Improve.ai is a movement intelligence coach that compares user motion to expert motion in real time and delivers actionable corrections and post-session analytics.

## Why this matters
- Traditional follow-along content is passive.
- Human coaching is high quality but expensive and low-frequency.
- Existing consumer fitness apps often provide shallow form feedback.

## Demo focus: Dance
In this version, Improve.ai targets dance training with:
- Real-time target-vs-user pose alignment
- Gamified motivation (XP, combo, progression)
- Useful corrective feedback (not generic encouragement)
- Replay-driven reflection to spot weak moments

## Platform thesis: General movement coaching
The same pipeline generalizes cleanly beyond dance:

### 1) Yoga and mobility
- Match asana form and hold stability
- Detect compensations and asymmetry over time
- Guide breath + posture pacing

### 2) Posture correction (office workers)
- Monitor sitting/standing alignment
- Detect rounded shoulders, forward head, and asymmetrical load
- Provide micro-break corrective drills

### 3) Physiotherapy and rehab
- Track ROM quality and exercise adherence
- Flag unsafe movement patterns
- Support clinician dashboards with progress trajectories

### 4) Sports technique analysis
- Compare athlete movement to ideal biomechanics
- Segment skill phases (setup, drive, follow-through)
- Build targeted drills around failure segments

## Defensible edge
- Multimodal movement scoring (landmarks + semantic descriptors + temporal trends)
- Feedback loop quality (real-time + post-session + AI narrative coach)
- Engagement mechanics that sustain consistency (game layer)

## Monetization paths
- Consumer subscription (personal coaching plans)
- B2B2C integrations (fitness studios, sports academies)
- Clinic and enterprise dashboards
- API for movement-quality scoring in partner apps

## 12-month roadmap
1. Add robust sequence alignment and move segmentation.
2. Integrate richer semantic representations (PoseScript-style textual motion descriptors).
3. Add coach dashboards and personalized progression plans.
4. Launch vertical-specific modules (dance, yoga, posture, rehab, sports).
